WHAT IS LUTEST

Marigold flower is one of the richest sources of Natural Carotenoids Lutein and Zeaxanthin. Lutein and Zeaxanthin has been repotted to be beneficial in several aspects to human health. It acts as protective shield against high energy blue light, key contributors to central vision as well as to high visual acuity, antioxidants that repair photo-induced oxidative damage and reducing the failure of the eyesight due to age related macular degeneration (AMD),coronary heart disease and cancer FREE.

ABOUT LUTEST SOLVENT FREE

  • LutEst Solvent Free is a Dark orange brown free- flowing viscous liquid.
  • It delivers 5% to 20% Lutein Esters in Oil and Powder.
  • Recommended dose - As Lutein 5 mg to 20 mg. As Lutein esters 10 mg to 40 mg
  • Storage - Store away from heat and light in an airtight container.
  • Shelf life - 2 years from the date of manufacture
